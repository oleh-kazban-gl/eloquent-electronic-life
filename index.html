Conventions
  • # - Barrier (walls & rocks)
  • o - Plant eater (eat plants, reproduce when has enough energy or/and other plant eater is near by)
  • @ - Tiger (eat the plant-eaters, fight with other tigers, reproduce when has enough energy or/and other tiger is near by)
  • * - Plant (give energy to plant eaters, reproduce when has enough energy)
